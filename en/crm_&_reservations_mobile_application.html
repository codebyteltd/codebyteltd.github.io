CRM & Reservations Mobile Application

The CRM & Reservations Mobile Application allows organizations to optimize communication and manage appointments efficiently through a solution of their brand. The app is tailored for businesses of any size, from SMEs to multinational companies with several shops, as is provided in a pay-as-you-grow model. The app is available for both Android and iOS devices.

Your business in a single app

A customer database in your hands

Access the customer database at any time from your phone or tablet. Communicate with your customers via phone, email or encrypted chat with one click.

Manage your appointments efficiently

Receive notifications about new appointment requests, respond to your customers and assign the appointments to your employees in no time.

Update your digital posture

Update the digital posture of your organization and automate your processes. Efficient communication will reduce the administrative burden and increase satisfaction among your customers.

Manage your business securely

The app was developed using state-of-the-art cybersecurity practices. Communication with your customers is stored in encrypted format, providing an extra layer of security for your business.

Features


Request appointments online

Appointment requests are submitted through the app using a user-friendly and self-explanatory appointment form.


Appointments management

Administrators are able to respond to appointment requests and assign appointments to the employees.


Employee calendar

Employees can view their calendars with the scheduled appointments for each day.


CRM Database

The CRM Database includes contact details of all customers, providing options for immediate communication.


Efficient communication

Communication is achieved efficiently with only one click, via phone, email or encrypted chat.


Encrypted chat

The encrypted chat of the app provides an ideal option for immediate and secure communication with your customers.


Notifications

The app provides various options for email and pop-up notifications and reminders.


Shop info

Publish information about your shops, such as maps, contact details, working hours and holidays.


Promotion of products and services

Promote the products and services of your organization, by sharing photos and the pricelist for each shop.

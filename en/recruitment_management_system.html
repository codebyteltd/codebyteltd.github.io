Recruitment Management System

Our Recruitment Management System (RMS) is a web platform designed to digitize and automate your recruitment process. The system allows you to easily create job postings, receive and track online applications, evaluate candidates through multiple phases of evaluation, and automate communication.

Recruitment made easy

Digitalize your recruitment process

The RMS will help you digitalize all phases of your recruitment process, manage the communication with the candidates efficiently and keep all data concerning the evaluation of candidates in a single place.

Attract more candidates

The RMS will enhance the digital posture of your organization and simplify the application process. Therefore, eligible candidates are more likely to be attracted and apply for the available positions.

Evaluate efficiently

The RMS will help you manage the evaluation of the candidates easily, step by step and in different phases, setting custom thresholds and evaluation criteria for each position.

Security and transparency

The RMS was designed and developed using state-of-the-art cybersecurity practices. The solution ensures the transparency of the evaluation process, preparing automated updates to the candidates about the progress.

Features


Digital proclamation

Administrators can easily proclaim a new job through the RMS, providing all the information that will be published, such as the number of available positions, the duties, and the expected qualifications of the candidates.


Online submission of applications

Applications are submitted online, using a user-friendly and self-explanatory application form. The progress on draft applications is saved during the process, allowing candidates to continue their application at a later stage.


Automated screening

The RMS incorporates an automated screening functionality, which checks whether candidates fulfil the basic qualifications for the position and makes recommendations concerning their eligibility to continue to the next evaluation phase.


Multi-phase evaluation process

The multi-phase evaluation process supported by the RMS contributes to the efficient, effective and transparent evaluation of the candidates based on the evaluation criteria set by the administrators.


Communication management

Automated email notifications are prepared to inform candidates regarding the progress of their applications. Moreover, candidates have the possibility to submit potential questions concerning their applications through the RMS.


Detailed reporting

The RMS provides the possibility to export applications with all their attachments in pdf format, as well as analytical reports and statistics concerning the current status of all applications for each position.
